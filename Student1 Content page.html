Sustainable Farming

The proportion of agricultural land under productive and sustainable agriculture is a crucial indicator for assessing the balance between food production and environmental and social sustainability. It considers land used for techniques like integrated pest management, organic farming, conservation tillage, crop rotation, agroforestry, and sustainable water management. This measure helps understand progress towards sustainable agriculture goals, which aim to ensure long-term food security, minimize environmental impact, reduce greenhouse gas emissions, conserve biodiversity, and improve livelihoods and social equity in rural areas. Achieving a high proportion of agricultural land under sustainable practices is essential for addressing global challenges like climate change, land degradation, and biodiversity loss.
